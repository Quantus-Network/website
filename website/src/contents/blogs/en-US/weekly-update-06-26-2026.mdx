The suits are getting Q-pilled.

This week Blackrock published a report on quantum computing and blockchains, and the Whitehouse issued an executive order accelerating their timelines for PQC migration.

Both confirm:

- Going post-quantum is an execution problem (no longer an ideological debate).
- Elliptic curve cryptography is at risk.
- NIST standards (ML-KEM, ML-DSA, and SLH-DSA) are the solution.
- The hard part is coordination.
- Migration needs to be completed in under 5 years.

There's over $2T at risk in the crypto markets, and it's our objective to secure as much of that as possible before Q-Day.
