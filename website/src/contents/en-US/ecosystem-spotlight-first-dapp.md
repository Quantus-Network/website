# Ecosystem Spotlight: QuantumSwap

We are thrilled to announce that **QuantumSwap** has successfully launched its beta on the Quantus testnet.

## What is QuantumSwap?

QuantumSwap is an Automated Market Maker (AMM) that leverages our quantum-secure signatures to ensure that liquidity providers are protected from future quantum threats.

### Features:
- **Low Slippage**: Optimized bonding curves.
- **PQC-Secured Pools**: Safety for long-term holders.
- **Integrated Wallet**: Smooth experience with the Quantus App.

The future of DeFi is here, and it's quantum-secure.
